Core platform

Three integrated layers powering AutoBio.ai.

LabOS & LabClaw

The first AI-XR Co-Scientist ecosystem linking dry-lab reasoning to wet-lab execution. Featuring a state-of-the-art 235B parameter lab-specialized Vision-Language Model (VLM) paired with AR/XR spatial tracking.

  • OpenClaw Native: Shift from "papers" to "skills"—making science executable, reproducible, and agent-native.
  • Multi-Agent Core: Self-evolving AI that plans, develops, evaluates, and controls robotic execution.
  • Secure Deployment: Local and enterprise-grade deployment for individual scientists or large automated hubs.

MedOS

An advanced AI operating system engineered to bridge complex biological data with actionable clinical insights. MedOS seamlessly translates the intelligence generated in the lab into therapeutic context.

  • Clinical Translation: Connects foundational biological discoveries directly to patient-centric outcomes.
  • Data Harmonization: Unifies multi-omic and clinical datasets into a single, queryable intelligence layer.

REVO Platform

A first-of-its-kind continuous evolution method for RNA and protein therapeutics design. REVO enables protein and RNA engineering at speeds and scales impossible with traditional methods.

  • Foundation AI: Powered by proprietary deep learning models for sequence-to-function mapping.
  • Continuous Experimentation: A closed-loop system that evolves designs through iterative, physical validation.
  • Context-Aware Assays: High-throughput biological screening seamlessly integrated with AI generation.
